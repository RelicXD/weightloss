Diet Plan: Underweight

You should make it a habit to eat more frequently; eat multiple smaller meals compared to one or two bigger meals each day.
Drinking too many liquids can fill you up and therefore they are not able to consume much food; you should try to drink after a meal and the drink should contain a higher calorie count.
When eating, don't be afraid choose extras to consume with your normal meal!
Finally, make sure to excercise, especially strength training will gain you muscles aswell as weight.


You should eat food with more nutrients such as:


  • Whole-grain Breads
  • Pasta
  • Cereals
  • Nuts and Seeds
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Dairy Products
  • Protein
  • Smoothies/Shakes
